Convert hexadecimal number 3F5 to decimal:

5 is in the 16^0 place (5 * 1 = 5)
F is in the 16^1 place (15 * 16 = 240)
3 is in the 16^2 place (3 * 256 = 768)
5 + 240 + 768 = 1013
